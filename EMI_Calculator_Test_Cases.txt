TC-SMK-01 | P0 | Verify that calculator loads and baseline home loan returns correct EMI for Rs. 10L at 10.5% for 10 years
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preconditions:
  - https://emicalculator.net/ is accessible
  - Chrome desktop browser, 1920x1080 resolution

Test Data:
  Tab:      Home Loan
  Amount:   Rs. 10,00,000
  Rate:     10.5%
  Tenure:   10 years

Steps:
    1. Open https://emicalculator.net/
    2. Confirm the Home Loan tab is selected by default
    3. Check loan tabs, input fields, sliders, results area, and pie chart are present
    4. Enter loan amount Rs. 10,00,000, interest rate 10.5%, and tenure 10 years
    5. Check Loan EMI, Total Interest Payable, and Total Payment in the results area
    6. Check the pie chart in the results area


Expected Result:
  The calculator loads with all controls visible. EMI is Rs. 13,493, Interest is Rs. 6,19,220, Total is Rs. 16,19,220, and the pie chart is present.
